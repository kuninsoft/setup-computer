For the system to work and have the power, you need to install the Power Supply and connect it to the motherboard.

Please refer to the Motherboard and Power Supply manual for connecting computer to the power. Use cables provided with power supply/motherboard.

Warning! Don't connect power supply to the electric grid when you still haven't mounted it and connected it. This is a device of an increased risk with high current in it, which may be an electric hazard.
